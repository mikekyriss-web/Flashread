Libby — Library Books
Free ebooks from your public library
FREE
How to import from Libby
1
Open the Libby app and borrow a book (choose EPUB format when offered)
2
Tap the book → tap "Read with…" → tap "Share EPUB"
3
Tap "Save to Files" → save it anywhere
4
Come back here and tap Load EPUB File below ↓
Note: Only EPUB format works — some library books are Kindle format. When borrowing, look for the EPUB/OverDrive option.

Amazon Kindle Books
Import books from your Amazon library
AMAZON
How to import Kindle books
1
On a computer, go to read.amazon.com and open your book
2
Use Calibre (free app at calibre-ebook.com) to convert your Kindle book to EPUB or TXT
3
Transfer the file to your iPad via AirDrop or iCloud
4
Load the file here using the button below ↓
Easier option: Many Kindle books can be borrowed for free from your library via Libby — search for the title there first!
